4. Data Retention & Deletion

Mission Data (Photos & Reports): All mission data, including photos and generated reports, are automatically deleted:

  • 7 days after mission completion (single mission users)
  • 30 days after mission completion (monthly subscribers)

Account Data: If you request deletion of your account, we will delete all associated data within 30 days, except where legally required to retain records.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage on Cloudflare R2
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted access to sensitive data
  • No data shared with third parties without your consent (except payment processors)

8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

GDPR (EU/UK): We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation. Your data is processed lawfully, with your consent, and you have full data subject rights.

CCPA (California): If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what data we collect, delete your data, and opt out of data selling (which we do not do).

9. Cookies

10. Children's Privacy

DetailRecon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.
