Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to generate a report?

Once you upload your photos, DetailRecon's AI auto-captions them and compiles everything into a professional PDF in approximately 15 minutes. No manual formatting required.

What happens to my data after I download the report?

All mission data is automatically deleted 7 days after download (or 30 days for monthly subscribers). We don't store your photos or reports longer than necessary. Your privacy is paramount in our design.

Can I use DetailRecon for multiple venues in one mission?

Yes. Each mission can include multiple venues. Upload all photos for each location, and the AI will organize them automatically with separate sections for each venue in your final PDF.

Is DetailRecon suitable for boutique security firms?

Absolutely. We offer Team Plans starting at ¬£499/month for small security firms with 5+ team members. White-label options are also available for firms wanting to embed DetailRecon into their own offering.

What file format is the report?

Reports are generated as professional PDF files. They can be downloaded, emailed, or shared via your preferred communication method. The formatting is optimized for screen viewing and printing.

Do I need special software or apps?

No. DetailRecon works entirely in your web browser. Upload photos from your phone or desktop, and download the PDF. No installation required. Works on any device with internet access.
